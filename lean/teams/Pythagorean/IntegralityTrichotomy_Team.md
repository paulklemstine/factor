# Research Team PHOTON-4: The Integrality Trichotomy

## Team Structure and Roles

---

### Principal Investigators

**Dr. Lyra Chen** — *Arithmetic Geometry & Formalization Lead*
- Expertise: Lorentz groups over integers, quadratic forms, Lean 4 formalization
- Role: Design and verify Lean proofs, establish the parity argument, formalize counterexamples
- Key contribution: The null cone parity theorem (η is always even)

**Dr. Marcus Okonkwo** — *Algebraic Topology & Division Algebras*
- Expertise: Clifford algebras, Hurwitz theorem, octonion geometry
- Role: Establish the connection between k−2 ∈ {1,2,4} and normed division algebras
- Key contribution: The Clifford algebra interpretation Cl⁺(5,0) ≅ M₂(ℍ) for k = 6

---

### Senior Researchers

**Dr. Elena Vasquez** — *Computational Number Theory*
- Expertise: Enumeration algorithms, descent methods, computational verification
- Role: Build and run computational verification infrastructure for k = 6 single-tree property
- Key contribution: Verified descent to root for all sextuples with d ≤ 50

**Dr. Kenji Tanaka** — *Modular Arithmetic & p-adic Analysis*
- Expertise: p-adic numbers, modular forms, barrier primes
- Role: Analyze mod-p descent and identify barrier primes for each failing dimension
- Key contribution: The barrier prime classification and mod-p descent conjecture

**Dr. Priya Sharma** — *Reflection Groups & Generators*
- Expertise: Discrete subgroups of O(n,1), Coxeter groups, generating sets
- Role: Investigate multi-reflection descent for k = 5
- Key contribution: Proved all uniform reflections fail; identified non-uniform candidates

---

### Postdoctoral Researchers

**Dr. Felix Brandt** — *Lean 4 Formalization*
- Role: Primary Lean coder, responsible for maintaining zero-sorry status
- Key contribution: Automated the parity proofs using `nlinarith` and `native_decide`

**Dr. Amara Osei** — *Visualization & Communication*
- Role: Create SVG visualizations, write Scientific American article
- Key contribution: The trichotomy diagram and division algebra correspondence table

### Graduate Students

**Sofia Martinez** — *k = 6 Descent Analysis*

**David Kim** — *k = 5 Alternative Mechanisms*
