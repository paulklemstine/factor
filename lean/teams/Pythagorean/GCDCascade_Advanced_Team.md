# GCD Cascade Advanced Research Team

## Team Structure

### Team Lead: Geometric Factoring Architect
**Role:** Oversees the connection between sphere geometry and integer factoring.
**Focus areas:**
- Channel GCD lattice structure
- Representation distance geometry
- Sphere parallelogram law and its factoring implications
- Coordinate systems on integer spheres

### Cascade Algorithm Developer
**Role:** Develops and optimizes the GCD Cascade algorithm.
**Focus areas:**
- Multi-representation cascade propagation
- Four-representation cascade networks
- Cascade sum identities
- Algorithmic complexity analysis

### Number Theory Specialist
**Role:** Develops the arithmetic foundations.
**Focus areas:**
- Prime factor channel dichotomy
- Channel product identities and simplification
- Factor orbit descent and arithmetic reduction
- Pell equation connections

### Higher-Dimensional Generalist
**Role:** Extends results to 4D, 5D, 6D and beyond.
**Focus areas:**
- General (n-1)d² channel sum formula
- Complementary channel pairs in 4D
- Cross-dimensional cascade comparison
- Dimensional trade-offs (more channels vs. harder representation finding)

### Formal Verification Engineer
**Role:** Maintains and extends the Lean 4 formalization.
**Focus areas:**
- Zero-sorry compilation of all theorem files
- Mathlib API integration
- Proof optimization and readability
- Axiom checking (#print axioms)

### Quantum Connections Researcher
**Role:** Explores quantum computing implications.
**Focus areas:**
- Phase estimation on integer spheres
- Grover-enhanced representation search
- Quantum cascade algorithms
- Connection to Shor's algorithm

### Cryptographic Analyst
**Role:** Assesses implications for cryptographic security.
**Focus areas:**
- RSA security analysis under cascade framework
- Lattice problem connections (SVP, CVP)
- Post-quantum cryptography implications
- Factor extraction complexity bounds
