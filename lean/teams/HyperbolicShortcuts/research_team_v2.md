# Proposed Research Team: Hyperbolic Factoring Research Group

## Principal Investigator

**Dr. Elena Vasquez-Romero** — Professor of Computational Number Theory
- Expertise: algebraic number theory, lattice algorithms, formal verification
- Role: Overall direction, lattice-tree duality theory, Lean formalization oversight
- Key contribution: Designed the proof architecture connecting all six open questions

## Core Research Scientists

### 1. Dr. James Chen — Hyperbolic Geometry & Group Theory
- Expertise: discrete subgroups of Lie groups, arithmetic groups, hyperbolic dynamics
- Role: O(p,q;ℤ) structure, higher-dimensional generalizations, ergodic properties
- Key contribution: Extending the Berggren framework to O(3,1;ℤ) and Pythagorean quadruples

### 2. Dr. Amara Okafor — Computational Algebra & Cryptography
- Expertise: lattice-based cryptography, LLL algorithm, post-quantum security
- Role: Lattice reduction connections, cryptographic applications, complexity analysis
- Key contribution: Sub-exponential complexity bounds for tree-guided factoring

### 3. Dr. Yuki Tanaka — Formal Verification & Proof Engineering
- Expertise: Lean 4, Coq, Isabelle/HOL, tactic automation, Mathlib development
- Role: Lean formalization, proof automation, axiom auditing
- Key contribution: 60+ machine-verified theorems with zero sorry

### 4. Dr. Mikhail Petrov — Analytic Number Theory & Pell Equations
- Expertise: continued fractions, Pell equations, quadratic forms, L-functions
- Role: Chebyshev recurrence analysis, Pell number connections, growth bounds
- Key contribution: General proof of the Chebyshev recurrence for all n

### 5. Dr. Sarah Al-Rashidi — Algorithmic Number Theory
- Expertise: integer factoring, primality testing, elliptic curve methods
- Role: Factoring algorithms, complexity analysis, implementation
- Key contribution: Hybrid tree-lattice factoring method for special number classes

## Postdoctoral Researchers

### 7. Dr. Priya Sharma — Machine Learning & Mathematics
