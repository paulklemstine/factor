### Key Discoveries

1. **The Cusp Form Barrier coincides exactly with the Hurwitz Boundary**
   - Pure Eisenstein ↔ composition algebra ↔ division algebra
   - Cusp correction ↔ zero divisors ↔ non-division algebra

2. **The Channel Dominance Principle**: Each Cayley-Dickson level carries more
   information than all previous levels combined (2ⁿ > 2ⁿ - 1)

3. **The Jacobi Identity for Ring Commutators**: Formalized using `noncomm_ring`,
   connecting Lie algebra structure to the Cayley-Dickson hierarchy

4. **Complete Zero-Divisor Exclusion Chain**: ℝ → ℂ → ℍ all formally proven
   to have no zero divisors, via norm multiplicativity

5. **Bott Periodicity at the Dimension Level**: 2^(n+8) = 2^n · 256,
   connecting to the 8-fold classification in topological physics
