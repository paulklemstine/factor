# Complexity Theory Research Team

## Mission

To develop machine-verified algebraic foundations for computational complexity theory, bridging tropical geometry, proof complexity, spectral methods, and parameterized algorithms through the unifying lens of idempotent algebra.

## Research Philosophy

1. **Verification First:** Every claim is machine-verified in Lean 4. We do not publish unverified results.

2. **Algebraic Unity:** We seek unifying algebraic principles (particularly idempotency) across complexity-theoretic frameworks.

3. **Open Infrastructure:** All formalizations, demos, and documentation are openly available.

4. **Theory-Practice Bridge:** Every theoretical result is accompanied by practical demonstrations and application analysis.
