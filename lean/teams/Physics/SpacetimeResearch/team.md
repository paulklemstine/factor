# Research Team: Formally Verified Spacetime Physics

---

## Mission Statement

To establish the first comprehensive, machine-verified mathematical foundation for spacetime physics, gravitational theory, and cosmology — bringing the rigor of formal proof to the deepest questions about the structure of the universe.

---

## Team Structure

### Principal Investigator
**Role:** Mathematical Physics & Formal Verification Lead
- Directs the overall research program
- Designs proof architectures connecting physics to formalization
- Ensures physical correctness and mathematical rigor

### Formal Methods Division

**Lean 4 Formalization Specialists (3 positions)**
- Translate physics theorems into precise Lean 4 statements
- Develop reusable Mathlib-compatible libraries for physics
- Create automation tactics specific to spacetime calculations
- Maintain the verified theorem library

**Proof Engineering Lead**
- Optimizes proof compilation and library organization
- Develops custom tactics for Minkowski geometry, tensor calculus
- Ensures compatibility with upstream Mathlib updates

### Physics Division

**General Relativity Specialist**
- Formalizes Lorentz geometry, causal structure, black hole thermodynamics
- Develops the fluid-gravity correspondence formalization
- Validates physical correctness of all gravitational theorems

**Cosmology Specialist**
- Formalizes CMB predictions from cosmic topology
- Develops the dimensional uniqueness arguments
- Models observational consequences of verified theorems

**Quantum Gravity & Information Specialist**
- Formalizes holographic error correction codes
- Develops the ER=EPR and Page curve formalizations
- Connects to quantum computing applications

### Applications Division

**Computational Physics Engineer**
- Develops Python demonstration codes
- Creates numerical validation suites
- Builds bridges between verified theorems and simulation codes

**Scientific Visualization Designer**
- Creates SVG and interactive visualizations
- Develops educational materials
- Produces publication-quality figures

**Science Communication Writer**
- Writes research papers and popular articles
- Manages outreach and public engagement
- Translates technical results for broad audiences
