## Research Roadmap

### Phase 1 (Completed): Foundations
- ✅ P-adic Möbius transformations
- ✅ Group structure (composition, inverse, determinant)
- ✅ Fixed-point theory and classification
- ✅ Ultrametric properties (isosceles, disk dichotomy)
- ✅ Conformal distortion formula
- ✅ Bruhat-Tits tree action
- ✅ 20 formally verified theorems, 0 sorries

### Phase 2 (6 months): Schottky Groups and Dynamics
- Formal Schottky group construction
- Limit set computation and properties
- P-adic Julia/Fatou theory
- Ergodic theory on the tree
- Target: 40 additional theorems

### Phase 3 (12 months): Berkovich Spaces
- Berkovich projective line
- Analytification functor
- Potential theory on Berkovich curves
- Target: 60 additional theorems

### Phase 4 (18 months): Langlands Connections
- Hecke operators on the Bruhat-Tits tree
- Principal series representations of GL₂(ℚ_p)
- Local Langlands for GL₂
- Target: 80 additional theorems
